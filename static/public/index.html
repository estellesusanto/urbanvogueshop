Mission

Our mission is to support you to be uniquely you. If you like to walk to the beat of your own drum, express yourself through the clothes you wear or consider yourself a trendsetter in one or more ways - then you've come to the right place. We collect one-of-a-kind vintage clothing and goods and present them to you so that you can be uniquely you.

Always Thrifted

We dive into thrifted bins, marketplaces, flea markets, boutique and consignment shops so you don't have to.

Always Honest

We will take high-definition pictures with extensive descriptions of all our products so that you know what you're getting before you buy it. Any deformities will be noted in the picture or description.

Always a Bargain

We want to be different than other small boutiques or consignment shops. We won't jack up the price just because it's one-of-a-kind. We will always try to price our items fairly.
